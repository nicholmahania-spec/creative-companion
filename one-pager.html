Creative Companion
Product one-pager · interview handout

Structure without shame.
Sparks without chaos.

A calm workflow companion for ADHD designers and photographers — tiny next moves beside Illustrator, Lightroom, InDesign, and Photoshop. Not another hustle app.

MVP web demo · data stays on device

Who it’s for

Primary

Solopreneurs and small studios who ship client craft, juggle Adobe tools, and freeze when the project feels too big.

Not for

Enterprise sprint theater, vanity streaks, or tools that punish unfinished lists.

MVP capabilities

Adaptive tasks

Name a job (café brand, wedding album). Get realistic phases and 5–15 minute steps.

Progress without pressure

Interactive map, dashboard, subtle phase chips. Partial progress counts.

Idea Spark

Rapid-Fire clouds, word chains, constraints. One tap → task or mood tile.

Mood + style lock

Drag-and-drop board, smart suggestions, gentle palette harmony — not a feed.

ADHD-first principles

  • Skip freely — phases and tasks never trap you
  • Enough today — stopping is a first-class action
  • Undo always — ⌘Z / Ctrl+Z for experiments
  • No guilt streaks — gentle wins, not fire badges
  • Beside Adobe — lightweight links first; deep UXP later

Brand system

Paper surfaces · teal accent · sage for done · serif headlines · calm density. Themes: Calm, Paper, Dusk, Dark, Ocean.

Why not Notion / Asana / Milanote alone?

Those tools are strong at docs, sprints, or pure boards. Companion is craft-aware micro-structure: loose idea → suggested next move → spark capture → enough for today — in the language of real creative jobs.
