# Redline v1.21.0 — Align product to 7-step design process

## Mandate
Nothing more / nothing less:

1. Define & Discover  
2. Research & Discovery  
3. Ideate & Brainstorm  
4. Sketch & Draft  
5. Design & Refine  
6. Review, Revise & Test  
7. Finalize, Deliver & Evaluate  

## Mapping

| # | Path label | View | Surface |
|---|------------|------|---------|
| 1 | Define | project | Goal, kits, readiness |
| 2 | Research | studio | Mood board / refs |
| 3 | Ideate | spark | Sparks (on-path) |
| 4 | Sketch | flow | One-step drafts |
| 5 | Design | brand | System artboard |
| 6 | Review | review | Checklist + readiness |
| 7 | Deliver | finish | Pack export |

## Removed as primary spine
- 5-stop Project/Work/Board/System/Pack labels  
- 4-mode clarify/structure/visual/refine process rail  

Tools (Timer, Calendar, Settings) remain off-path.
